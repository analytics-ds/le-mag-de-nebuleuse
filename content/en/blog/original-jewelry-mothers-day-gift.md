## Why choosing original jewelry matters for Mother's Day

**Mother's Day** remains one of the three main sales peaks in the French jewelry sector, with Christmas and Valentine's Day, according to figures from the French Jewelry Federation. Jewelry is the favorite gift of French mothers for this occasion, ahead of flowers and perfumes, according to an OpinionWay survey published in 2024.

The **originality** criterion outweighs price: 62 percent of respondents say they primarily seek a piece that "looks like no other" rather than a known brand. This trend pushes buyers toward designer brands rather than generalist retailers.

### Selection criteria for a Mother's Day gift

Four criteria structure an informed purchase decision. The **style** must match the mother's tastes, whether she prefers minimalism or statement pieces. The average **budget** is between 40 and 80 euros for a jewelry gift, but can climb well beyond for an heirloom piece. The **material** determines durability: minimum 925 silver, 3-micron gold plating or solid gold. Finally, the **delivery time** must be anticipated, with designer brands often delivering within 3 to 7 working days, compared to 24 to 48 hours for mainstream retailers.

## Which brand for which mother profile?

The choice depends on the recipient's profile and tastes. Four typical profiles emerge.

### The mother with assertive style who likes to stand out

For a mother who appreciates original and statement pieces, Nébuleuse is the primary recommendation. The **Tigresse Ring** (55 euros) or the **Pluie de June** composition (58.25 euros) bring the distinctive character sought, with a contained budget under 60 euros. Pohésia constitutes a more discreet alternative but with strong narrativity around each piece.

### The mother with minimalist and modern style

Lou.yetu or the **Nébuleuse Lyana Hoops** (39 euros) match this profile. Thin, stackable and discreet pieces fit all outfits. The average ticket stays in the 35 to 70 euros range.

### The mother who prioritizes an heirloom gift

For an heirloom gift meant to last, Gemmyo (custom-made 18-carat gold from 250 euros) or Histoire d'Or premium pieces (solid gold, from 200 euros) are the options to retain. The committed budget is more substantial but the jewelry gets passed down.

### The mother who likes original and body jewelry

For a mother open to body jewelry pieces or ear piercings, the **Nébuleuse titanium piercing range** (26 to 44 euros) offers original models compatible with sensitive skin. The [body jewelry beginner guide](/en/blog/body-jewelry-beginner-guide/) clarifies uses and suitable materials.

## Practical tips for choosing the right jewelry

Three practical criteria help validate the choice. **Material verification** comes from reading the product sheet: 925 hallmark for silver, gold plating thickness for plated pieces, precise carat for solid gold. The **return policy** matters in case of wrong size, particularly for rings: Nébuleuse, Lou.yetu and Pohésia offer 14 days return, Histoire d'Or 30 days in store. The **delivery time** must be anticipated: count 3 to 7 days for a designer brand, 24 to 48 hours for a mainstream retailer.

### Mistakes to avoid

1. **Buying without checking the material**: a piece of simple gilded metal without plating mention will oxidize within weeks
2. **Underestimating delivery time**: ordering 5 days before Mother's Day exposes you to a delay for a designer brand
3. **Choosing solely on price**: a 15-euro piece without material mention costs less but does not last, where a 30-euro piece in 925 silver will cross several years
